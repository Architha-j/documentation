Facebook Comments is an integration that allows you to connect your Facebook business account or page to Yellow.ai.

With this integration, you can achieve the following:
1. Add a comment to a post or reply to user comments.
2. [Creates a new ticket](https://docs.yellow.ai/docs/platform_concepts/studio/build/nodes/action-nodes#17-raise-ticket) for a comment in the Inbox module. Any responses received to the comment will be added to the same ticket.

    ![](https://i.imgur.com/xBSLtHy.png)
    
   
:::note 
* In Inbox, you can view user comments, but not the details of the user who posted the comment.
* If multiple users reply to a comment, their responses are shown in the same conversation.
* When the same user adds a new comment, it is considered as a new ticket.
:::

## Connect Facebook Comments to Yellow.ai


To connect Facebook Comments to Yellow.ai, you need to have a Facebook Business account with at least one Facebook page created.



### Link your Facebook Business account

To link your Facebook business account, follow these steps:

1.  On the switcher, click **Channels**.

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/kv01RDY.png)
   
2. Navigate to **Social** and enable **Facebook comments** to connect to your Facebook account or page.   

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/X983fbO.png)

3. Login to your Facebook account that is connected with your Facebook Business Page.

   <center><img src="https://i.imgur.com/47XbbF2.png" alt="drawing" width="60%"/></center>

4. After logging in, click **continue** to navigate to your Facebook page.

   <center><img src="https://i.imgur.com/e6z2lqv.png" alt="drawing" width="60%"/></center>


5. Select your desired business page and click **Connect**. You can connect multiple pages.

   <center><img src="https://i.imgur.com/o4bt7xF.png" alt="drawing" width="60%"/></center>
   
:::note
If you have more than one business page connected to this Facebook account, select the one for which you want to setup a chatbot.
:::
   
Then, [test the bot](#) to ensure your comments are displayed in that particular Facebook page.

  
## Test your Facebook comments chatbot

After creating your Facebook account successfully, you can test the chatbot with any of the existing flows.

Test your Facebook comments chatbot, follow these steps:

1. [Setup the bot](https://docs.yellow.ai/docs/platform_concepts/Getting%20Started/create-a-bot) based on your business use case.
2. Open your Facebook business account and navigate to your business page.
3. Add a sample comment from a different account or see if there is any new comment from your users.
